I am a dedicated student of the blade and lover of the asphalt. Rollerblading has been near and dear to my heart ever since I first saw the amazing tricks people can do with just a pair of rollerblades and a little determination. While rollerblading has left me frequently bloodied, battered, and broken, I always come back for more. The sport has made me tougher physically and mentally, and even though it's nearly killed me I can't ever say no to a skate sesh with a few buddies.
