Introduction

Rollerblading is one of the coolest ways to travel, and a genuine joy to do. While sometimes it might be scary, mostly it's just a fun activity you can do either with your friends or alone. Come along with me as we explore magic of slappin blades on your feet and rippin down pretty much any concrete or asphalt surface!

Materials Needed

  • Blades - Available online or at used sports/thrift shops
  • Pads - Good for beginners and veterans alike
  • Buddies - There to pick you up when you fall
  • Bandages - You gotta learn how to eat it and eat it hard
  • Snacks - Keep your blood sugar from dropping

Instructions

  1. Strap on your rollerblades
  2. Stand up
  3. Don't fall over (can be helped by holding on to something; a car, a bush, ect.)
  4. Start moving one foor in front of the other
  5. Begin gliding through your walking motions
  6. Pick up some steam
  7. That's it! You're blading!
